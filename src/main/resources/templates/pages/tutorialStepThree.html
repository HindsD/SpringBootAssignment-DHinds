Step Three

Set Attainable Goals

Setting attainable goals for yourself is one of the most important pieces of advice I can give to you. Everyone has their own level of fitness that they want to achieve.

If you’re trying to get fit, know that your goals are different than someone else’s. Make goals that are suitable for your needs and that are attainable. If you make goals that are highly improbable, you’ll end up being disappointed with the outcome.

A goal of yours can be as simple as feeling better during a workout. It doesn’t have to be drastic by any means, but if you make goals that are attainable, if will feel amazing when you accomplish them.
