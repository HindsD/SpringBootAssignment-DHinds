Step Two

Find a Workout Buddy

Working out can be a pain at times, especially if you have difficulty keeping yourself accountable for working out. If you don’t have a trainer or fitness instructor pushing you to workout, then I highly recommend finding someone to workout with.

Having a workout buddy will help you keep each other accountable for working out. You’re more likely to push yourself harder during the workout if you have someone working out with you that has similar goals as yourself.
