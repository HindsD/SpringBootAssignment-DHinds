Step One

Workout Consistently

Getting fit starts with committing to working out. It’s imperative that you create a schedule for working out that you stick to. You won’t make any progress overnight, and unfortunately, a lot of people become discouraged if they aren’t seeing any results initially.

As time goes on, you will begin to notice changes in your physical fitness. Fitness isn’t gaged by appearance either — remember that. Don’t evaluate your fitness based on how you look. Rather, base your fitness off of how you feel and the difficulty of your workouts.

If initially you are only able to run one mile, but a few weeks later you’re able to run two or three miles, you have made progress and are in better shape. Appearance doesn’t determine success.
